More Specific About AI Works

AI used in Input detection is a combination of CNNs, GNNs and RL
CNNs (convolutional neural network): scan images, recognise patterns;
GNNs (general neural network): understand relationships between elements
RL (reinforcement learning): learn from mistake, use in filtering inputs

Processing machine integrate multi-agent AI
Neuro-symbolic systems (like IBM's Neuro-symbolic AI) learns and reasons, prevents any fatal attemps

Further Explainations and Credits

The core mechanics to all this AI Integrations is rather simple, yet ambitous but not impossible. It all started with the idea of having AI to assist you in a certain field, which I chose healthcare. It also caught my attention that patients often past away owing to the lack of organ donors, more specifically compatible ones. Of course, there have been solutions to this matter, most well-known being artificial organs. However, the afore-mentioned method faces a variety of concerns due to high-manufacture cost and limited production. Then I thought, what if AI could help solve that problem? Then I came up with this idea: a hybrid between artificial and organic, supervised and interfered by AI. The concept is as follow: Initially I would want platform-based AIs (like IBM Watson Health) as they have an existing large database. With said database I could enhance the learning process of my AI, so it could recognize what is the input, how to react, and it could also detect strange objects. To put it simply, have it learn based on real experiences andt theory. Moving on, I initially thought to implement it right away, however after considering ethnical and governal issues, I suggest beginning with small applications, such as prothestic limbs. This way, fatalities are reduced and you could more easily detect bugs. After a period of gaining trust, we shall begin implementing said AI into organ, but quite similarly only on a small scale first. About energy resources, harvesting Glucose based on Oxidation or Energy from Currents using superconductors might work but as for out current energy, it is not impossible but should takes about 10-20 years. Deeper AI influence should begin once we figure out a more efficient way to harvest energy inside your body while not killing any cells. To recapitulate briefly, the idea is about having AI interfere with artificial organ to improve its efficiency and quality, whilst harvesting energy from cells. Anticipated to be popularized in about ten years
